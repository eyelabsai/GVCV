I'm a refractive surgeon because clear vision changes lives, and because patients deserve a straight, careful path to it.

I'm a fellowship-trained ophthalmologist specializing in vision correction and cataract surgery — an expert in EVO ICL, SMILE, LASIK, PRK, CTAK, and laser cataract surgery. I trained in refractive surgery at Parkhurst NuVision under Dr. Gregory Parkhurst — principal investigator of the FDA EVO ICL trial and one of the country's highest-volume EVO ICL surgeons.

My work centers on education, innovation, and excellence in refractive surgery, with a personal commitment to helping patients understand their possibilities and achieve their best vision. Every patient gets a direct answer: the choices that genuinely fit them, whether they're a strong candidate, the real risks, and what to expect. If it isn't the right time to operate, I'll say so plainly.

Beyond the clinic, I build the tools other surgeons rely on: I'm the co-founder and lead engineer of ICL Fit, the leading AI-based ICL-sizing agent, and a creator of IOL Reference, the intraocular lens database trusted by cataract surgeons worldwide — and a published researcher in ICL sizing and vault prediction.

My care doesn't stop at the operating room — I look after my patients well beyond it.

“I have EVO ICLs of my own — sized with the AI tool I helped build. Living without glasses or contacts is one thing; understanding, first-hand, the decision my patients are weighing is another.”
